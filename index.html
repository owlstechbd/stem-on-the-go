About Us

About Us

STEM on the Go is a unique enrichment program that provides interactive STEM experiences to engage learners from all cultural and economic backgrounds. We facilitate standards-based learning opportunities that foster collaboration and inclusion. Our program encourages critical thinking and creativity while exploring the areas of engineering, environmental sustainability, and health awareness.

Our Mission

Our Mission

STEM on the Go strives to expose students to fun and engaging standards-based science lessons that promote curiosity, confidence, and problem-solving skills.

Our Vision

STEM on the Go envisions a future of STEM related careers that reflect an equitable balance of gender and racial diversity.

Mentoring Program

JOIN US for Cookies, Cocoa, and Conversations! Yes, a casual informal time to hear an inspirational message, ask questions, chat, set goals, check-in, collaborate, and more. Topics include STEM related:

  • Movies
  • Books
  • Construction development sites
  • Health Facility Visits
  • Shadow work days
  • Excursions
  • Guest Speakers
